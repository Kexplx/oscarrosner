CrimeView

A web app which analyses a route based on a Polygon, Line or Radius for 1 - 3 german cities and displays all districts (de: Stadt- Landkreise) with their respective crime rate (# of offences per 100.000 inhabitants) on it.

CrimeView combines data from three different sources:

  • Opendatasoft - Geojson of districts
  • Google Places - City predictions and details
  • AWS Lambda - Crime statistics
