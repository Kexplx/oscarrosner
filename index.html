Projects

Crimeview

A web app which analyses a route based on a Polygon, Line or Radius for 1 - 3 german cities and displays all districts (de: Stadt- Landkreise) with their respective crime rate (# of offences per 100.000 inhabitants) on it. CrimeView combines data from three different sources:

  • Opendatasoft (GeoJSON of districts)
  • Google Places (City predictions and details)
  • Bundeskriminalstatistik 2020 (Crime statistics)

Live version, GitHub Repository
